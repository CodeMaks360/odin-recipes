Ground Beef Bulgogi

Description

Want to taste something oriental? Try this easy, fast and cheap to make dish.

Ingredients - 4 people

  • Ground beef - 455g
  • Garlic, crushed - 5 cloves
  • Minced ginger - 1 Tbsp OR 1/2 tsp of dried ginger
  • Sesame oil - 2 tsp
  • Soy sauce - 120ml
  • Brown sugar - 75g
  • Crushed red pepper flakes 1/4 tsp
  • Scallion, choopped - 1 bunch

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef and break it. Cook until browned. Drain off excess grease.
  2. Over medium heat add the garlic, fresh ginger and sesame oil. Cook for about 2 minutes
  3. Stir in soy sauce, brown sugar and crushed red pepper flakes. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook uncovered until the sauce reduces (about 5-7 minutes). Stir in the scallion
  4. Serve immediately over white rice and garnish with preferred toppings
