German style sausages

Description

Never went to Germany? Don't worry! Those sausages will bring a piece of Deutsch to your Land!

Ingredients - 4 people

  • Sausages - 4
  • Olive oil - as needed

Steps

  1. Put sausages into a pot of water and bring to a boil. Simmer gently for about 10 minutes
  2. Drain sausages and brush with olive oil
  3. Grill over a medium heat barbecue or a grill until browned and done
  4. And done! Serve with yellow mustard for some spiciness
